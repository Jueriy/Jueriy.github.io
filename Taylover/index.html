Know Something about TAYLOR SWIFT

An 11-timeGrammy Winner , Taylor Swift is one of the most prolific and celebrated artists of her generation. She is the only female artist in history to win the music industry’s highest honor, the Grammy Award for Album of the Year, three times. Her many awards and distinctions include being the most awarded artist ever in American Music Awards’history along with being named Artist of the Decade ; winner of the BRIT Awards International Female Solo Artist of the Year in 2015 and the Global Icon Award in 2021; She is the only solo artist this century to have three albums reach No .1 in one year .
